Introduction

I'm Alessa Cross, a third-year at the University of Chicago pursuing a B.S. in Computer Science. I am Japanese and Australian. I was born and raised in the heart of Tokyo until I turned 14 years old, when I decided to attend a boarding school, Phillips Academy in Andover, Massachusetts.
I first started programming in October 2017. While I've just started my journey in the world of computing, I have worked on several projects and taught myself a few languages so feel free to take a look at what I've done so far. I've built this website so there is physically more space and freedom (than in a page-long resumé) to share what I've worked on and express with more nuance my passion for materializing incredible ideas using code. I am excited to continue adding to this page and to build cooler projects with more beautifully organized code with each passing day.
