Hi, I’m Ben. I’m currently privacy engineer at Apple, where I work on integrating privacy into Apple's products and services.

Previously, I was an undergraduate at the University of Chicago, where I worked on research with the UChicago SUPERgroup focused on understanding what people think about the inferences used for web personalization and ad targeting, and exploring how we can provide more transparency to users about tracking and personalization.
